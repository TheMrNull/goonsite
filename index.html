About us

“Get Gooned.” We’re not just a party; we’re a collective descent into the delightfully unsettling. The Gooner Party began as a simple, absurd idea – a space to embrace the weird, the uncomfortable, and the wonderfully gooey. We believe in shedding societal expectations and celebrating the inherent strangeness within us all. Our gatherings are intentionally unstructured, guided by impulse and a shared desire to… well, goon. There are no rules, no judgments, only the opportunity to explore the possibilities that emerge when inhibitions are let loose. We’re a group of individuals drawn together by a mutual appreciation for the unexpected. We encourage experimentation, creative expression, and a willingness to fully immerse yourself in the present moment. “Join the Goon.” If you're searching for a space where curiosity reigns supreme, where the pursuit of pure, unadulterated strangeness is actively encouraged, then you've found your place. We welcome anyone ready to embrace the chaos, the slime, and the beautiful, bizarre potential that lies within. Note: This is an ongoing experiment. Expect the unexpected. Participation is voluntary... but highly recommended.
